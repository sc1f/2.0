About Me

I'm Jun Tan.

I'm a developer, designer, photographer, and junkie for all things visual and political.

I'm a sophomore at the University of Texas at Austin, studying Plan II Honors and Government. I have extensive experience in full-stack development, visual design, communications/PR, data journalism, photography, and digital content production.

Here are some of the hats I currently wear:

  • Incoming enterprise business solutions intern at Textron, a Fortune 500 aerospace and defense conglomerate
  • Communications Intern at UT-Austin Dell Medical School
  • Student Technology Assistant at UT-Austin LAITS

I formerly worked as a digital producer at The Texas Democratic Party, served as the technical operations manager at The Daily Texan, and twice as a public speaking instructor at Harvard Debate Council's summer workshops.
